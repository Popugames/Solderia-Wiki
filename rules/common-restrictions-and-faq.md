# Common Restrictions and FAQ

## Can I leave an alliance while my troops are in a rally?
Not freely if the rally has already launched. In that case, your leave may be blocked or delayed until the rally is resolved.

## Can I leave an alliance while my troops are in the Palace?
Normally not if doing so would leave the Palace garrison in an invalid state.

## Can I join a rally after it launches?
No. After launch, the rally composition is locked.

## Can I cancel a rally after it launches?
No. A rally can normally be cancelled only while it is still forming.

## Can I use the same troops for two actions?
No. Troops assigned to one valid action are unavailable for other actions until that action ends.

## Can the Wishing Well replace gathering and city production?
No. It is a bonus system, not the main source of economy.
