# Action Limits and Cooldowns

## Why limits exist
Limits and cooldowns protect game balance and stop abuse.

## Common examples
The game may use limits or cooldowns for:
- rejoining alliances
- number of active marches
- number of active rallies
- refresh attempts
- extra Wishing Well uses
- teleport usage
- protection states

## Rule
If a limit or cooldown is active, the related action cannot be performed until the condition is cleared.
