# Refresh Rules

## Overview
The Wishing Well may allow players to refresh the current reward offer.

## Types of refresh
A refresh may be:
- free, usually limited per day
- paid with premium currency
- event-based or item-based in future versions

## What refresh does
A refresh replaces the current visible reward offer according to the active reward rules.

## Restrictions
Players cannot:
- refresh infinitely without meeting the cost or limit
- keep claiming from an invalid or outdated reward state
- bypass daily refresh caps

## Important note
Refreshes do not create unlimited value. Reward quality and quantity remain controlled by the system.
