# Wishing Well Overview

The **Wishing Well** is a city system that gives players controlled bonus rewards over time.

## Purpose
The Wishing Well is designed to:
- reward regular logins
- provide small bonus value
- add a recurring utility loop
- support progression without replacing the main economy

## What it is not
The Wishing Well is not meant to replace:
- city production
- gathering
- research
- proper progression

It is a bonus system, not the main source of growth.

## Unlock
The Wishing Well is unlocked through city progression, usually at a required Castle level or through a guided quest.
