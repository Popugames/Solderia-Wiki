# Charges and Rewards

## Charges
The Wishing Well uses **charges** or limited attempts.

A charge is one available use of the Wishing Well.

## Recovery
Charges recover over time until the maximum stored amount is reached.

## Rewards
Rewards may include:
- resource bundles
- speedups
- utility items
- event currency
- other controlled bonus rewards

## Reward selection
Depending on the final design, the Wishing Well may:
- give a random reward
- show several reward slots and allow one selection

## Rule
The Wishing Well must use server-generated rewards. Rewards cannot be generated only on the client.
