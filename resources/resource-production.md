# Resource Production

## City production
Resource buildings generate resources over time.

## Production rule
Production continues while you are offline, but it may be limited by storage capacity depending on the system rules.

## What affects production
Production may be affected by:
- building level
- research
- active buffs
- Palace ownership bonuses
- temporary event bonuses

## What is allowed
You may:
- improve production by upgrading buildings and research
- combine city production with gathering

## What is not allowed
You may not:
- collect more passive production than the system allows if storage is full
- gain production bonuses without a valid source
