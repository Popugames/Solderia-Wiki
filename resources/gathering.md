# Gathering

## Overview
Gathering is the act of sending troops to resource nodes on the world map.

## How it works
1. select a valid resource node
2. send troops
3. troops travel to the node
4. they gather resources over time
5. they return to your city with the collected amount

## What affects gathering
Gathering efficiency may be affected by:
- troop load
- march size
- march speed
- gathering bonuses
- node size and type

## What is allowed
You may gather only from valid resource nodes.

## What is not allowed
You may not:
- gather from an invalid or empty node
- collect more than your troops can carry
- use troops that are already assigned to another action
