# Resources Overview

Resources are the foundation of city growth and military progression.

## Main resources
The main basic resources are:
- Food
- Wood
- Iron

A separate premium currency may also exist.

## How resources are used
Resources are spent on:
- building upgrades
- troop training
- research
- healing
- other utility actions

## How resources are obtained
Resources come from:
- city production
- gathering on the world map
- quests
- rewards
- item packs
- special systems such as the Wishing Well

## Rule of importance
Resources are meant to be important, but the game should not rely on a single source of income.
