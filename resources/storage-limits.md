# Storage Limits

The current storage table is driven by the **Depot** building. Its values define how much protected resource storage the city can hold for each resource type.

## Depot benchmarks

| Depot level | Power | Food | Wood | Iron | Mithril |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 70 | 270,000 | 270,000 | 45,000 | 11,200 |
| 5 | 1,390 | 290,000 | 290,000 | 48,300 | 12,000 |
| 10 | 12,430 | 340,000 | 340,000 | 56,600 | 14,100 |
| 15 | 41,720 | 440,000 | 440,000 | 73,300 | 18,300 |
| 20 | 121,530 | 540,000 | 540,000 | 90,000 | 22,500 |
| 25 | 291,190 | 640,000 | 640,000 | 106,600 | 26,600 |
| 30 | 609,340 | 740,000 | 740,000 | 123,000 | 30,800 |

## Notes

- Food and Wood share the same Depot values at every listed benchmark.
- Iron and Mithril have lower protected limits, so late-game storage still needs planning around upgrades and troop training.
- Resource items stay flexible because they are stored as inventory items until opened.
