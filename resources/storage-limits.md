# Storage Limits

## Overview
Your city does not only need production. It also needs enough capacity to hold what you produce.

## Warehouse function
The Warehouse protects and expands your storage capacity.

## Rule
If a resource system uses storage caps, passive production cannot continue beyond the allowed limit.

## Why storage matters
Without enough storage:
- your passive resource gain is reduced
- large upgrades become harder to prepare
- you may waste production time

## Restrictions
Storage limits do not usually block item-based rewards unless a separate inventory or mail rule applies.
