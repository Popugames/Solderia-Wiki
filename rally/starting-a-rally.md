# 🚩 Starting a Rally

## Overview
Only players with the proper alliance permissions can start a rally.

Starting a rally is the first step in organizing a coordinated alliance attack.

---

## 👤 Who Can Start a Rally
A rally can only be started by a player who:

- is in an alliance
- has the required rally permission
- has troops available
- selects a valid target
- is not blocked by any active rally limit

In most cases, this means the action is reserved for alliance leadership or trusted members.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works
When a rally is created:

1. a target is selected
2. a formation timer begins
3. alliance members can join before launch

The formation timer gives the alliance time to gather troops before the attack begins.

---

## 🕒 Rally Timer
During the formation phase:

- players may join the rally
- players may prepare their troop contribution
- the rally waits for launch

Once the timer ends, the rally starts moving automatically.

---

## ✅ Requirements
To start a rally, the player must:

- be in an alliance
- have the required permission
- have troops available
- select a valid target
- respect any rally limits that apply

---

## ⛔ What Is Not Allowed
A player cannot start a rally:

- without permission
- without available troops
- against an invalid target
- if a blocking rally limit has already been reached

---

## 💡 Tips for Players

### Start rallies with enough time
Give alliance members time to notice the rally and join properly.

### Pick the right target
Rallies should be used for important objectives, not wasted on weak or low-priority attacks.

### Coordinate before launch
A rally is much stronger when the alliance is ready before the timer starts.
