# Joining or Leaving a Rally

## Joining a rally
A player may join a rally only while it is still in the **formation phase**.

## What happens when joining
When a player joins:
- troop selection is locked into the rally
- those troops are no longer available for other actions

## Leaving before launch
A player may leave a rally while it is still forming.

If they leave before launch:
- their reserved troops are released
- the rally totals are recalculated

## After launch
Once the rally has launched:
- players can no longer join
- players can no longer leave manually
- the troop composition is locked

## Why this rule exists
A launched rally is treated as one coordinated action. Its state must stay stable until it resolves.
