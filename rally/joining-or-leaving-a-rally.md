# 🪖 Joining or Leaving a Rally

## Overview
Players may join a rally only while it is still forming.

Once the rally launches, participation becomes locked until the action is resolved.

---

## ➕ Joining a Rally
A player may join a rally only during the **formation phase**.

### What Happens When Joining
When a player joins:

- troop selection is locked into the rally
- those troops are no longer available for other actions
- the rally totals update based on the new contribution

---

## ➖ Leaving Before Launch
A player may leave a rally while it is still forming.

If they leave before launch:

- their reserved troops are released
- the rally totals are recalculated
- those troops become available again

---

## 🔒 After Launch
Once the rally has launched:

- players can no longer join
- players can no longer leave manually
- the troop composition is locked

This rule exists to keep the rally stable during movement and combat.

---

## ❓ Why This Rule Exists
A launched rally is treated as one coordinated military action.

If players could freely join or leave after launch, it would create unfair situations and break the rally’s combat logic.

---

## 💡 Tips for Players

### Join early
Do not wait until the last seconds if you want to make sure your troops are included.

### Double-check your troops
Once you join, those troops are reserved and cannot be used elsewhere.

### Be ready before launch
After the rally starts moving, no manual changes are allowed.
