# Rally Rules and Restrictions

## What is allowed
Players may:
- create a rally if they have permission
- join a rally during the formation phase
- leave a rally during the formation phase
- cancel a rally before launch if they are allowed to do so

## What is not allowed
Players may not:
- join a rally after it launches
- leave a rally after it launches
- cancel a rally after it launches
- use troops already committed to a different action
- leave the alliance freely while they lead or participate in a launched rally if that would break the rally state

## Special cases
If a player tries to leave or is removed from the alliance while they are still tied to an active rally, the alliance change may be delayed until the rally is fully resolved.
