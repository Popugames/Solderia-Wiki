# Rally Overview

A **rally** is a coordinated alliance attack started by one player and joined by others.

## Purpose
Rallies allow multiple alliance members to combine troops against a common target.

## Typical targets
Rallies are especially important for:
- attacking the Palace
- future alliance objectives
- large shared targets

## Why rallies matter
Some strategic objectives are not meant to be taken by one player alone.

## Core rule
A rally has a formation phase, a launch moment, and a locked combat journey after launch.
