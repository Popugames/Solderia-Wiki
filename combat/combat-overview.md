# Combat Overview

Combat determines the outcome of attacks against monsters, rival players, or important structures.

## General rule
Combat is resolved according to the final strength of the attacking and defending sides, including all valid bonuses and restrictions.

## What affects combat
Combat may be influenced by:
- troop count
- troop type and tier
- research
- city bonuses
- Palace buffs
- temporary event or item buffs
- target type

## Outcomes
A battle may result in:
- victory
- defeat
- wounded troops
- dead troops
- rewards or no rewards
- battle reports

## Important note
The exact formula may be hidden, but the rules of eligibility and result must remain consistent.
