# Battle Reports

## Overview
Battle Reports show the result of a completed battle.

## Typical contents
A report may contain:
- attacker
- defender
- target type
- winner
- troop losses
- wounded troops
- rewards or loot
- time of battle

## Purpose
Reports help players understand:
- what happened
- why a battle was won or lost
- how many troops were affected

## Rule
Reports reflect completed server-side outcomes. They are not predictions.
