# Buffs and Bonuses

## Overview
Buffs are modifiers that improve or sometimes limit the effectiveness of troops or systems.

## Common sources
Buffs may come from:
- research
- buildings
- items
- Palace control
- events

## Examples
Buffs may affect:
- attack
- defense
- health
- march speed
- gathering speed
- construction speed
- healing speed

## Important rule
Only active and valid buffs apply. A buff is not active simply because it exists in the game; it must come from a source currently affecting your account or alliance.
