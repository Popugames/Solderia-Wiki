# 📝 Joining an Alliance

## Overview

Joining an alliance is one of the most important early steps in kingdom progression.

An alliance gives you access to coordinated gameplay, shared objectives, and strategic content that solo players cannot use effectively.

---

## 🚪 Ways to Join

Depending on the alliance settings, you may join in one of the following ways:

### Open Join
If the alliance is open, you can join instantly.

### Application Approval
If the alliance requires approval, you must submit an application and wait for a Leader or Officer to accept it.

---

## ✅ Requirements

You can join an alliance only if all normal conditions are met.

Typical requirements include:

- you are not already in another alliance
- you are not blocked by a rejoin cooldown
- you meet any entry conditions required by that alliance

Some alliances may also prefer players with certain activity levels, power, or language preferences.

---

## ✔️ What Is Allowed

You may join or apply to an alliance if:

- you are alliance-free
- you meet the alliance conditions
- you are not restricted by cooldown or game rules

---

## ⛔ What Is Not Allowed

You may not:

- be in more than one alliance at the same time
- bypass alliance join restrictions
- ignore cooldown rules after leaving or being removed

---

## 🌟 Why Joining Early Matters

Players in active alliances usually progress faster because they gain:

- stronger coordination
- access to rally content
- better support during events and wars
- more opportunities to participate in important objectives

---

## 💡 Tips for Players

### Choose an active alliance
A smaller but active alliance is usually better than a large inactive one.

### Read the alliance description
Some alliances focus on war, some on growth, and some on casual play.

### Do not leave casually during big events
If you leave at the wrong time, you may be unable to rejoin another alliance immediately.
