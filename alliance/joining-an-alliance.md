# Joining an Alliance

## Ways to join
Depending on the alliance settings, a player may:
- join directly
- submit an application
- be accepted by leadership

## Requirements
A player must:
- not already be in another alliance
- not be blocked by a rejoin cooldown
- meet any alliance entry rules that may exist

## What is allowed
A player may apply or join if all alliance conditions are met.

## What is not allowed
A player may not:
- be in more than one alliance at a time
- bypass join restrictions
- ignore cooldown rules after leaving or being removed
