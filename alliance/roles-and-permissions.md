# Alliance Roles and Permissions

## Typical roles

### Leader
The alliance leader has the highest authority and can manage the alliance.

### Officer
Officers help manage members and alliance actions.

### Member
Members participate in alliance activities and follow alliance leadership.

## Common permissions
Depending on rank, players may be allowed to:
- approve applications
- remove members
- start rallies
- send alliance notices
- manage alliance settings

## Important rule
Permissions are not based only on UI visibility. If a rank does not have permission, the action cannot be performed.
