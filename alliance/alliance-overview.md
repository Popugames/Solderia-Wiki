# Alliance Overview

An alliance is a player group that enables coordination, shared progression, and access to alliance-based objectives.

## Why alliances matter
Alliances allow players to:
- communicate and organize
- participate in rallies
- defend and attack important objectives
- contest the Palace

## Basic structure
An alliance contains:
- a leader
- officers
- members
- permissions based on rank

## Important rule
Some of the most important endgame systems in Solderia cannot be used effectively without an alliance.
