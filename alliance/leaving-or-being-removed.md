# Leaving or Being Removed from an Alliance

## Leaving an alliance
A player may normally leave an alliance from the alliance menu.

## When leaving is restricted
A player cannot freely leave if one of the following is true:
- they are the alliance leader and leadership has not been transferred
- they are leading an active rally
- they are participating in an active rally that has already launched
- their troops are stationed in the Palace garrison
- they are involved in another alliance-critical action that has not been resolved

## Being removed
Alliance leadership may remove a member if the member is eligible to be removed under the current rules.

## Delayed leave or removal
If a player is still tied to active alliance troop actions, their removal may be delayed until those actions finish or their troops are returned.

## Important rule
Alliance changes must not leave troops in an invalid or ownerless state.
