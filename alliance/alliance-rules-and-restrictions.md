# Alliance Rules and Restrictions

## What alliance members can do
Alliance members may:
- use alliance chat
- participate in rallies
- support alliance goals
- defend alliance objectives

## What alliance members cannot do
Alliance members cannot:
- hold multiple alliance memberships
- leave while still bound by restricted alliance actions
- use alliance systems without the required permissions
- occupy alliance-only objectives alone if those systems require alliance participation

## Leadership restrictions
Leaders cannot:
- leave without transferring leadership
- dissolve the alliance while major alliance-controlled objectives are still active, if the game rules block it

## Cooldowns
After leaving or being removed, a player may be subject to a rejoin cooldown before entering another alliance.
