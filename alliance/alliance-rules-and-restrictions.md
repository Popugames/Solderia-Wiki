# 📜 Alliance Rules and Restrictions

## Overview
Alliance systems include a few important restrictions to keep gameplay fair and prevent abuse during strategic actions.

These rules mainly affect alliance changes, rally participation, and Palace troop management.

---

## What Alliance Members Can Do
Alliance members may:

- use alliance chat
- participate in rallies
- support alliance goals
- defend alliance objectives

---

## What Alliance Members Cannot Do
Alliance members cannot:

- hold multiple alliance memberships
- leave while still bound by restricted alliance actions
- use alliance systems without the required permissions
- occupy alliance-only objectives alone if those systems require alliance participation

---

## Leadership Restrictions
Leaders cannot:

- leave without transferring leadership
- dissolve the alliance while major alliance-controlled objectives are still active

---

## Cooldowns
After leaving or being removed, a player may be subject to a rejoin cooldown before entering another alliance.

This helps reduce abuse during wars and major strategic events.

---

## Why These Restrictions Exist
These rules help prevent:

- broken rally states
- unfair alliance switching during important events
- Palace troop abuse
- invalid battle outcomes

They also make alliance gameplay easier to understand and more stable for everyone.

---

## Tips for Players

### Plan alliance changes ahead of time
Do not wait until rallies or Palace conflicts are already active.

### Coordinate with leadership
If you want to leave or reorganize during an event, speak with your alliance leaders first.

### Check restrictions before acting
Some actions may be blocked temporarily because your troops are still committed.
