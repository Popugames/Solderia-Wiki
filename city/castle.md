# Castle

The **Castle** is the most important building in your city.

## Purpose
The Castle represents your overall progression stage. It is the main gate for:
- unlocking buildings
- raising maximum building levels
- accessing stronger systems
- increasing your account progression

## How it works
As your Castle level increases, more features become available. Many other buildings and systems are tied to it.

## Requirements
Castle upgrades usually require:
- resources
- upgrade time
- specific support building levels

## Rules
You can upgrade your Castle only when all listed requirements are met.

## Restrictions
You cannot:
- upgrade the Castle without enough resources
- upgrade the Castle if required buildings are below the required level
- exceed the Castle level cap of the current game version

## Design consequence
The Castle is the main progression anchor, but it is never meant to be upgraded in isolation.
