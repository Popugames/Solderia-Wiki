# City Overview

Your city is the center of your progression.

Everything you do in Solderia depends on the development of your city:
- building growth
- resource generation
- troop training
- research
- support for map activity

## Main city functions
Your city allows you to:
- construct and upgrade buildings
- produce and store resources
- unlock systems
- increase troop capacity and strength
- prepare for alliance warfare

## City progression rule
A city does not progress only through one building. You must improve multiple structures to support stronger Castle upgrades.

## What players can do
Players can:
- view and manage buildings
- start upgrades when requirements are met
- use speedups where allowed
- improve their city over time even while offline

## What players cannot do
Players cannot:
- start an upgrade without meeting requirements
- bypass building dependencies
- use the same builder or queue slot for multiple actions if only one slot is available
