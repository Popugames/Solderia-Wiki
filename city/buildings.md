# Buildings

Buildings are divided into several categories.

## Main categories

### Core building
- Castle

### Resource buildings
- Farm
- Sawmill
- Mine

### Military buildings
- Barracks
- Hospital
- Wall

### Knowledge and support
- Academy
- Warehouse
- Wishing Well

## Building purpose
Each building exists for a specific reason:
- production
- training
- healing
- research
- storage
- utility

## Rules
A building may be:
- locked
- available
- active
- upgrading

## Restrictions
A building cannot perform more than one incompatible action at the same time.
