# Speedups

Speedups reduce the remaining duration of compatible timed actions.

## Typical uses
Speedups may be used on:
- building upgrades
- troop training
- research
- healing

## Rules
A speedup reduces the remaining time of an active action.

If the remaining time reaches zero, the action completes immediately.

## Restrictions
Speedups cannot be used:
- on actions that are already complete
- on actions that are not active
- on incompatible targets

## Important note
A speedup changes time remaining, not the rules of the action itself. Requirements and costs still apply.
