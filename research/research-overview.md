# Research Overview

Research improves your economy and military performance.

## Main categories
- Economy Research
- Military Research

## Why research matters
Research is one of the main permanent progression systems in Solderia. It improves efficiency instead of simply increasing raw quantity.

## How it works
Research takes time and usually requires:
- an Academy
- resources
- previous research levels or other conditions

## Important rule
Research bonuses apply only after the research finishes successfully.
