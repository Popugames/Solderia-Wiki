# Military Research

Military Research improves the strength and efficiency of your armies.

## Typical examples
Military Research may improve:
- troop attack
- troop defense
- troop health
- training speed
- march speed
- rally performance

## Purpose
It increases your ability to win battles, gather more safely, and participate in alliance warfare.

## Important rule
Military Research is one of the main factors that separates similar troop counts into stronger and weaker armies.
