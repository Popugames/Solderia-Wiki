# Main Goals

## Primary goal
Your main progression goal is to increase your **Castle level** and unlock more of the game's systems.

## Secondary goals
To reach that goal, you must also:
- improve your resource income
- upgrade support buildings
- train troops
- complete research
- use the world map efficiently

## Endgame goal
The long-term alliance objective is to **capture and hold the Palace**.

## Important design rule
The Palace is not an early-game feature. It is an alliance objective intended for stronger players and coordinated groups.
