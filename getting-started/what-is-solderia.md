# What is Solderia

**Solderia** is a 4X strategy game focused on city growth, army progression, map activity, alliance coordination, and control of key objectives.

The game is built around two layers:

## 1. Your city
Your city is where you:
- construct buildings
- produce resources
- train troops
- research upgrades
- strengthen your overall power

## 2. The world map
The world map is where you:
- gather resources
- hunt monsters
- move armies
- support your alliance
- fight for strategic objectives such as the **Palace**

## Long-term progression
Your city power increases through:
- higher Castle level
- stronger buildings
- better research
- larger and stronger armies
- active alliance participation

## Endgame direction
A strong player eventually joins or leads an alliance, participates in **rallies**, and helps capture and defend the **Palace**.
