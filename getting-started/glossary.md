# Glossary

## Castle
The main building of your city. It determines your overall progression stage.

## March
A troop movement from one point to another on the world map.

## Rally
A coordinated alliance attack created by one player and joined by other alliance members.

## Garrison
Troops stationed inside a structure for defense.

## Palace
A central alliance objective that can be occupied and defended.

## Charge
A stored use of the Wishing Well.

## Wounded troops
Troops that survive combat but must be healed before they can fight again.
