# 👑 Palace

## Overview
The **Palace** is one of the most important strategic objectives in the kingdom.

Only one alliance can control the Palace at a time. Because of that, Palace wars are some of the largest and most important alliance battles in the game.

---

## Why the Palace Matters
Controlling the Palace gives an alliance access to powerful strategic advantages.

The Palace is meant to be:

- a major alliance objective
- a reason for large-scale coordination
- a symbol of kingdom dominance
- a long-term competitive goal for top alliances

Some of the most important kingdom conflicts will revolve around Palace control.

---

## How the Palace Works
The Palace is not meant to be taken by one player alone.

To fight for the Palace, alliances must coordinate attacks through **Rally** and defend it with a proper **Palace garrison**.

In most cases, Palace control follows this flow:

1. the Palace becomes available
2. alliances launch rallies to attack it
3. the defending force is defeated or holds its ground
4. control goes to the winning alliance
5. the new owner reinforces the Palace and tries to hold it

---

## Main Palace Systems
The Palace system is built around three core parts:

### ⚔️ Attacking the Palace
Alliances compete for the Palace through coordinated attacks.

### 🛡️ Palace Garrison and Control
The alliance that controls the Palace can defend it by stationing troops inside.

### 📜 Palace Rules and Restrictions
Special rules exist to prevent abuse and keep Palace battles fair.

---

## Important Rule
The Palace is an **alliance objective**, not a solo objective.

Players are expected to coordinate with their alliance when attacking, defending, reinforcing, or recalling troops.
