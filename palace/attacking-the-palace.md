# Attacking the Palace

## Basic rule
The Palace is attacked through alliance action, not as a normal solo target.

## How it works
A qualified alliance member starts a rally on the Palace.
Alliance members join the rally.
When the rally launches and arrives:
- it fights the current Palace defense
- if it wins, control changes to the attacking alliance

## Requirements
A player usually needs:
- alliance membership
- required permission or access through a rally
- enough troops
- the minimum progression stage if such a rule exists

## What is not allowed
Players cannot:
- attack the Palace in a way that bypasses rally rules if Palace attacks are alliance-only
- use invalid or unavailable troops
- expect the Palace to remain uncontested after capture
