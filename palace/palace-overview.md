# Palace Overview

The **Palace** is the main alliance objective of the kingdom.

## Purpose
The Palace exists to create endgame competition between alliances.

## Why it matters
The alliance that controls the Palace gains strategic value, status, and active benefits.

## Main rule
The Palace is not a personal objective. It is an **alliance objective**.

## Core gameplay loop
To control the Palace, alliances must:
- grow strong enough to contest it
- launch rallies
- defeat its defenders
- maintain control against future attacks
