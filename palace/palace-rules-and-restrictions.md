# Palace Rules and Restrictions

## What is allowed
The owner alliance may:
- hold the Palace
- reinforce the Palace garrison
- benefit from active Palace bonuses
- defend it against enemy rallies

Enemy alliances may:
- launch rallies to contest it
- try to take control by defeating its defense

## What is not allowed
Players may not:
- reinforce the Palace if they are not in the owning alliance
- freely withdraw troops during restricted battle states if the system blocks it
- leave alliance systems in an invalid state by removing troops incorrectly

## Membership restriction
A player normally cannot leave their alliance freely while their troops are still stationed in the Palace garrison if that would create an invalid troop or ownership state.

## Control rule
If the Palace changes owner, old Palace benefits end and the new alliance's control begins.
