# Troop Overview

Troops are required for map activity, combat, gathering, and alliance warfare.

## Main troop classes
The basic military classes are:
- Infantry
- Archers
- Cavalry

## Troop progression
Troops become stronger through:
- higher tiers
- better research
- city progression
- temporary or permanent buffs

## Uses
Troops can be used to:
- attack monsters
- gather resources
- join rallies
- defend structures
- occupy the Palace

## Important rule
Troops are never meant to exist only as a number. They are always part of a state:
- available
- training
- marching
- garrisoned
- wounded
