# Troop States

A troop group may exist in several states.

## Available
These troops are ready to be used.

## Training
These troops are not yet active and cannot be deployed.

## Marching
These troops are traveling on the map and cannot be used elsewhere.

## Garrisoned
These troops are stationed in a defensive structure.

## Wounded
These troops survived combat but must be healed before they can be used again.

## Dead
These troops were lost in combat and are no longer available.

## Important rule
The same troops cannot exist in two states at the same time.
