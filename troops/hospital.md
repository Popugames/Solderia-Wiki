# Hospital

## Overview
The Hospital stores and heals wounded troops.

## Purpose
The Hospital reduces the impact of combat losses and makes repeated fighting more sustainable.

## How it works
After certain battles, some troops become **wounded** instead of being lost immediately. These troops can be healed over time.

## What affects healing
Healing may be affected by:
- Hospital capacity
- healing time
- resource cost
- healing bonuses
- speedups

## Restrictions
If wounded troops exceed capacity, overflow rules may apply depending on final balancing.

## What is allowed
You may heal wounded troops if you have capacity and resources.

## What is not allowed
You may not use wounded troops in combat before they are healed.
