# Troop Training

## Overview
Troops are trained in military buildings such as the Barracks.

## How it works
When you start training:
- resources are consumed
- a training timer begins
- the troops are added only when the timer completes

## Requirements
Training usually requires:
- the correct building
- sufficient resources
- unlocked troop type or tier

## What is allowed
You may:
- train troops if you meet the requirements
- speed up training if the system allows it

## What is not allowed
You may not:
- use troops still in training as active troops
- train troop types you have not unlocked
- start training without enough resources
