# Marches

March capacity currently starts at **1 active march slot**. March size is then scaled through **Drill Grounds** upgrades.
| Drill Grounds level | Power | March size |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 40 | 3,000 |
| 5 | 950 | 11,600 |
| 10 | 8,490 | 24,600 |
| 15 | 28,450 | 40,100 |
| 20 | 82,970 | 58,100 |
| 25 | 198,810 | 78,600 |
| 30 | 416,040 | 101,600 |
